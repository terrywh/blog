> 地址映射：https://mirrors.tencent.com/github.com/{group}/{repo}/

参考以下示例：
* 用户：[https://mirrors.tencent.com/github.com/terrywh](https://mirrors.tencent.com/github.com/terrywh)
* 项目：[https://mirrors.tencent.com/github.com/jekyll/jekyll](https://mirrors.tencent.com/github.com/jekyll/jekyll)
* 下载：[https://mirrors.tencent.com/github.com/shadowsocks/shadowsocks-rust/releases/download/v1.17.1/shadowsocks-v1.17.1.x86_64-unknown-linux-gnu.tar.xz](https://mirrors.tencent.com/github.com/shadowsocks/shadowsocks-rust/releases/download/v1.17.1/shadowsocks-v1.17.1.x86_64-unknown-linux-gnu.tar.xz)

注意：
由于实际映射地址中的链接并未进行变更，会发生跳转回 github.com 或 404 not found 的情况；
